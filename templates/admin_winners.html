Winner Selection Guide
How Winner Selection Works:
  1. Performance-Based Rewards: Rewards are calculated based on kills and final position
  2. Position Rewards:
    • 1st Place: 60% of prize pool + kill bonuses
    • 2nd Place: 30% of prize pool + kill bonuses

  3. Kill Bonuses: Additional coins per kill (varies by position)
  4. Manual Selection: Admins select winners based on game results
